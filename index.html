Rating and Price Distibution of Restaurants listed on Zomato.

The map shown below highlights the average rating (out of 5) of all the restaurants listed on Zomato. Green shades represent a rating close to 5, whereas Red shades represent a rating close to 1. Clicking on any country shows the price-wise distribution of restaurants in that particular country. The countries in 'grey' do not have any restaurants listed on Zomato.
